Taking logs gives

\[ \log(weight) = \log(\alpha) + \beta \log(length) \]

which we can rewrite as:

\[ \log(weight) = \beta_0 + \beta_1 \log(length) \]

The above formula should be of very familiar form to you by now. Provided that we make the assumption that \(Îµ ~ N(0, Ïƒ)\) then this isprecisely the simple linear regression model with response variable \(log(weight)\) and explanatory variable \(log(len)\).
